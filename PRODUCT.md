# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

TCG collectors and casual players in the Netherlands and surrounding regions. They browse on mobile during commute and desktop at home. The job: find a specific card or discover new singles/sealed product, check condition and price, buy with confidence, and track orders.

## Product Purpose

TCGHaven is an online trading card game shop selling singles, sealed products, and accessories across Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Magic: The Gathering, and more. It exists to give collectors a fast, trustworthy alternative to cluttered legacy TCG sites and faceless template stores. Success looks like repeat customers who trust the inventory accuracy and enjoy browsing.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, playful, reliable. The shop feels like walking into your favorite local card store — welcoming, a little fun, but you trust them to grade and ship properly. The voice is warm and direct, never corporate.

## Anti-references

- **Cluttered TCG sites**: walls of tiny text, overwhelming filter panels, no breathing room, 2005-era layouts. TCGHaven should feel spacious and modern.
- **Generic Shopify stores**: template-driven, stock photos, no personality. TCGHaven should have a distinct identity that collectors recognize.

## Design Principles

1. **Card-first**: the product is the star. Every layout decision serves browsing, comparing, and buying cards.
2. **Breathe**: generous whitespace and clear hierarchy. No walls of text or cramped grids.
3. **Trust through clarity**: prices, conditions, and stock are always unambiguous. No guessing.
4. **Personality without noise**: playful touches (color, micro-interactions, copy) that don't slow the shopper down.
5. **Fast by default**: performance is a design choice. Light assets, minimal JS, instant feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Standard contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, screen reader support. Respect prefers-reduced-motion and prefers-color-scheme where applicable.
