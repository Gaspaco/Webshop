# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Independent musicians, bedroom producers, and beat-makers who create music in the browser. They're working from laptops, often late at night, in personal creative flow. The job: make music without installing heavy desktop software.

## Product Purpose

MeloStudio is a browser-native DAW (digital audio workstation). It lets creators produce, arrange, and share music entirely in the browser. Success looks like a creator going from idea to published track without leaving the tab.

## Brand Personality

Bold, Raw, Underground. Like an indie label that takes its craft seriously but doesn't polish away the edge. Confident without being corporate. The interface should feel like a creative tool made by musicians, not a SaaS product made by a growth team.

## Anti-references

- Gamified UI: no badges, XP bars, achievement unlocks, progress rings, or leveling systems
- Generic SaaS: no hero-metric templates, identical card grids, or "streamline your workflow" copy
- Over-polished corporate: no enterprise-gray, no safe-and-boring

## Design Principles

1. **Show the artist, not the app** — the creator's identity and work should dominate, not chrome and controls
2. **Dark stage, bright spotlight** — dark backgrounds with deliberate, high-contrast moments of color
3. **Tactile, not flat** — UI should feel like it has weight and texture, like physical studio equipment
4. **Confidence over explanation** — trust the user to figure it out; don't over-label or over-explain

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support prefers-reduced-motion. Ensure all interactive elements have visible focus states. Text contrast 4.5:1 minimum on all surfaces.
